What is Flock?

Make genuine local friends through shared events and experiences

How it works

1
Tell us what you're into

Football, gym, Indian food, gaming, hiking, live music — whatever makes you you. The more you share, the better your matches.

2
Set your preferences

Choose your city, how far you're willing to travel, and who you'd like to meet — girls, guys, or everyone.

3
Get matched to events

Flock finds events in your city that match your interests. These are real, organised events planned in major cities across the UK.

4
Show up and meet your flock

Go to the event, meet people who are into the same things, and make genuine local friendships that last.

"When people go to these events they will hopefully make local friends who are into the same sort of things as them."
